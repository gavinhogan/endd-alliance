THE STRATEGY

The Strategy

To deal maximum damage, your strongest squad must fight as much as possible. If your 1st squad starts a rally, it sits idle for at least 3 minutes. Instead, use your 2nd squad to start a rally to "hold a spot" for others to join with less wait time. Use your 1st squad to join the rally that is leaving the soonest.

Healing and Help

Constant healing is required. Only heal small batches of troops (30-minute timers). Click the Help request button as soon as your batch starts. If everyone clicks the "Help" icon constantly, your troops will heal for free in seconds. This keeps our alliance's total damage high.

Location

You should be at the alliance hive. If you are far away, you might be removed from rallies to save time. If you cannot teleport back, you should skip the exercise.
