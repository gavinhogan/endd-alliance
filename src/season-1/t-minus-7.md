<span class="season-section-title">1. STAMINA MANAGEMENT</span>

Please stop burning your stamina inventory today and keep them for Season 1.

*   **Why:** When Season 1 starts, every single commander will need to independently initiate rallies against **Doom Elites**. Securing "First Blood" on **30 different Elites** is how you get the Mutant Crystals and Immunity Proteins required to jump-start your Virus Research Institute. Additionally, we will be running conquests on lands just like we did at the start of the game, which will also consume significant stamina.

<span class="season-section-title">2. SPEEDUPS (HEALING, BUILDING, AND TRAINING)</span>

Season 1 introduces new buildings and new combat mechanics. Your stash of speedups is your tactical safety net.

*   **Building Speedups:** On Day 1, you will unlock brand-new facilities like the **Virus Research Institute** and **Protein Farms**. Instead of waiting on construction timers, use your building speedups to instantly rush these facilities. This lets you bypass the early bottleneck and start generating seasonal resources immediately.
*   **Healing Speedups:** Season 1 introduces the **RA Virus**. When attacking corrupted targets like Doom Elites or Strongholds, your troops will take extra damage if your Virus Resistance isn't high enough yet. Even playing it safe, expect a spike in wounded troops. Having a large pile of healing speedups means you can instantly recover your squads and get back to the action without being benched.
*   **Training Speedups:** While healing is the priority, there will be some inevitable troop losses as we expand our territory. Hoard your training speedups to ensure your main squads are always topped off for big rallies.

Season 1 requires a lot of map movement. We will be relocating frequently to capture Strongholds, group up at Outposts, and support each other. The slow drip of free teleports won't be enough to keep everyone grouped up comfortably.

Having a healthy stash means you'll never be stuck far away from the action or out of range when **CptHarry**, **LtHarryBz**, or the rest of our leadership team sets a rally point.
