<span class="section-title">I. THE STRATEGIC BOTTLE-NECK</span>

Unlocking Tier 9 troops is the single most important power inflection point for our alliance dules and server defense. However, T9 upgrades are incredibly resource-heavy and require strict discipline to execute. 

This guide outlines our alliance's official directives on T9 transition plans to prevent officers from wasting resources during Season 1.

<span class="section-title" id="priorities">1. UPGRADE PRIORITY TREE <button class="share-intel" onclick="copyIntel('priorities')"></button></span>

- **🛡️ Priority 1: Tank Tech (The Frontline):** The vast majority of our server uses tank squads. You must focus on tank tech first to stay competitive.
- **🛡️ Priority 2: Barracks Leveling:** Do not upgrade all barracks to 26 concurrently. Focus on a single barracks to level 26 to unlock T9 tanks, then rotate to Tech Center leveling.
- **🛡️ Priority 3: Tech Requirements:** Under "Special Forces", you need significant points in "Squad I" and "Squad II" before T9 unlocks are authorized.

<span class="section-title">III. RECRUITING & DRILL MANAGEMENT</span>

- 🏥 **Hospital Buffs:** Ensure hospital capacity is at least 250k before launching T9 battles. T9 troops are extremely expensive to retrain if they overflow and die.
- 🤝 **Waterfall Promotions:** Promote T8 troops to T9 during the Arms Race/VS Training window on Friday for maximum points. Do not train T9 from scratch if you have T8s sitting idle—always promote first to save stamina and resources.
