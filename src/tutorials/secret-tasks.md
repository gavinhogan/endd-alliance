- **Target Other Servers:** Travel to an enemy server and look near their **Capitol**. That is where the high-power players with Gold tasks are located. Note: During an inter-server battle you will want to plunder on the specific server we are up against.
- **The "Perfect Zoom" Technique:** If you zoom out too far, tasks disappear from the map. Zoom in too close, and it takes too long to find them. Once you find a task, use it to set your zoom level to the maximum height where it is still visible. This is the fastest way to scan the enemy map.
